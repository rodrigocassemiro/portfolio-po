# User Story - Digital Contract Signature

## User Story

As a tenant,

I want to sign my rental agreement digitally,

So that I can complete the rental process remotely and securely.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1

Given that my contract is ready

When I access the document

Then I should be able to review all contract details before signing.

### Scenario 2

Given that I agree with the contract terms

When I complete the digital signature process

Then the contract should be legally signed and stored.

### Scenario 3

Given that the signature process is completed

When the contract is finalized

Then all involved parties should receive a copy of the signed document.

## Business Value

- Faster contract completion
- Reduced operational costs
- Improved customer experience
- Secure digital process
- Increased transaction efficiency
