# WhatsApp Authentication Platform

## Overview

The objective of this initiative was to reduce onboarding friction by allowing customers to access the platform using WhatsApp as their primary authentication method.

Traditional registration processes often create unnecessary barriers, increasing abandonment rates and reducing conversion opportunities. The proposed solution leveraged WhatsApp as a trusted identity channel to simplify user access and accelerate engagement.

---

## Business Context

The real estate rental journey involves multiple interactions before a customer reaches the contract stage.

Analysis identified that requiring users to complete lengthy registration forms before interacting with properties created significant drop-off rates during the early stages of the funnel.

The business needed a faster and more intuitive onboarding experience capable of increasing conversion while maintaining secure access control.

---

## Problem Statement

Customers were abandoning the platform before completing registration due to excessive onboarding friction.

This resulted in:

* Lower lead conversion rates
* Increased user abandonment
* Delayed customer engagement
* Reduced efficiency in the acquisition funnel

---

## Product Goal

Enable users to authenticate using WhatsApp and gain immediate access to the platform with minimal effort.

---

## Discovery Process

### Stakeholder Interviews

Conducted discussions with:

* Business leadership
* Operations team
* Customer service team
* Technology team

### Key Findings

* Registration was perceived as a barrier
* Users preferred familiar communication channels
* WhatsApp was already the primary customer interaction channel
* Existing authentication flows created unnecessary complexity

---

## Product Hypothesis

We believe that enabling WhatsApp-based authentication will reduce onboarding friction and increase user conversion rates.

Success will be measured through:

* Registration completion rate
* Conversion rate
* User activation rate
* Time-to-first-interaction

---

## MVP Scope

### Included

* WhatsApp authentication
* User identity validation
* Automatic account creation
* Session management
* CRM synchronization

### Excluded

* Social login providers
* Multi-factor authentication
* Advanced profile customization

---

## User Journey

1. User accesses the platform
2. User selects WhatsApp login
3. Platform validates identity
4. Account is created or identified
5. Session is established
6. User accesses marketplace features
