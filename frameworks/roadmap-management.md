# Roadmap Management Framework

## Objective

Provide a structured approach to define, prioritize and communicate product initiatives aligned with business goals, customer needs and technical feasibility.

## Roadmap Principles

* Align with business strategy
* Focus on customer value
* Prioritize outcomes over outputs
* Maintain flexibility for change
* Balance innovation and operational stability

## Roadmap Planning Process

### 1. Strategic Alignment

Activities:

* Business objective review
* Executive stakeholder alignment
* Market opportunity assessment
* Product vision validation

Outputs:

* Strategic goals
* Product themes
* Success criteria

---

### 2. Opportunity Identification

Sources:

* Customer feedback
* Product analytics
* Operational challenges
* Market research
* Competitive analysis
* Regulatory requirements

Outputs:

* Opportunity backlog
* Problem statements

---

### 3. Prioritization

Evaluation Criteria:

* Business impact
* Customer impact
* Strategic alignment
* Risk reduction
* Revenue potential
* Delivery effort

Methods:

* MoSCoW
* RICE
* Impact vs Effort Matrix

Outputs:

* Prioritized initiatives
* Roadmap candidates

---

### 4. Roadmap Construction

Roadmap Levels:

#### Strategic Roadmap

Focus:

* Long-term vision
* Product themes
* Business outcomes

#### Tactical Roadmap

Focus:

* Quarterly initiatives
* Product releases
* Major milestones

#### Delivery Roadmap

Focus:

* Sprint planning
* Feature delivery
* Execution tracking

Outputs:

* Product roadmap
* Release roadmap
* Delivery roadmap

---

### 5. Stakeholder Communication

Audience:

* Executive leadership
* Product team
* Engineering team
* Operations team
* Business stakeholders

Communication Cadence:

* Monthly roadmap review
* Quarterly planning
* Release updates
* Strategic reviews

Outputs:

* Roadmap presentations
* Status reports
* Decision records

---

### 6. Continuous Review

Triggers:

* Market changes
* Customer feedback
* Business priorities
* Technical constraints
* New opportunities

Activities:

* Roadmap reassessment
* Initiative reprioritization
* Scope adjustments

Outputs:

* Updated roadmap
* Revised priorities

## Success Metrics

* Roadmap predictability
* Initiative completion rate
* Customer impact
* Business value delivered
* Stakeholder satisfaction
* Strategic goal achievement

## Product Owner Responsibilities

* Define roadmap priorities
* Align stakeholders
* Manage trade-offs
* Communicate roadmap status
* Adapt roadmap to changing conditions
* Ensure alignment with product vision

## Expected Outcome

A transparent and outcome-driven roadmap that aligns stakeholders, maximizes business value and guides product development toward strategic objectives.
