# Product Discovery Framework

## Objective

Provide a structured approach to identify customer problems, validate opportunities and define solutions before development begins.

## Discovery Principles

* Understand the problem before proposing solutions
* Validate assumptions with data
* Focus on customer needs
* Prioritize business outcomes over features
* Reduce delivery risk through early validation

## Discovery Process

### 1. Problem Identification

Activities:

* Stakeholder interviews
* Customer interviews
* Support analysis
* Operational pain point mapping
* Market observation

Outputs:

* Problem statement
* Opportunity definition

---

### 2. Research & Validation

Activities:

* User research
* Competitor analysis
* Market analysis
* Behavioral data analysis

Outputs:

* User insights
* Business opportunities
* Validation report

---

### 3. Hypothesis Definition

Template:

"We believe that solving [problem] for [customer] will result in [expected outcome]."

Example:

"We believe that enabling WhatsApp authentication will reduce onboarding friction and increase customer conversion rates."

Outputs:

* Product hypotheses
* Success assumptions

---

### 4. Solution Design

Activities:

* User journey mapping
* Process mapping
* MVP definition
* Solution workshops

Outputs:

* User flows
* MVP scope
* Initial roadmap

---

### 5. Prioritization

Evaluation Criteria:

* Customer impact
* Business value
* Strategic alignment
* Technical feasibility
* Delivery effort

Methods:

* MoSCoW
* RICE
* Impact vs Effort Matrix

Outputs:

* Prioritized backlog
* Roadmap candidates

---

### 6. Success Metrics Definition

Examples:

* Conversion rate
* User engagement
* Adoption rate
* Revenue impact
* Customer satisfaction

Outputs:

* KPI framework
* Product success metrics

## Discovery Deliverables

* Problem Statement
* User Personas
* User Journey Map
* Product Hypotheses
* MVP Definition
* Business Case
* Prioritized Backlog
* Product Roadmap

## Product Owner Responsibilities

* Lead discovery activities
* Align stakeholders
* Define product hypotheses
* Prioritize opportunities
* Validate business value
* Ensure customer-centric decision making

## Expected Outcome

A validated product opportunity with clear business value, customer demand and measurable success criteria before development investment begins.
