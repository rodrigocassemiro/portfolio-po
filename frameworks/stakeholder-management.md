# Stakeholder Management Framework

## Objective

Ensure alignment between business, technology and operations throughout the product lifecycle.

## Stakeholder Categories

### Executive Leadership
- Strategic direction
- Business goals
- Investment decisions

### Product Team
- Product vision
- Prioritization
- Roadmap management

### Engineering Team
- Technical feasibility
- Architecture decisions
- Delivery execution

### Operations Team
- Process validation
- User feedback
- Adoption support

### Customers
- Needs and expectations
- Product feedback
- Satisfaction metrics

## Communication Cadence

### Weekly
- Team alignment
- Sprint activities

### Biweekly
- Sprint Review
- Sprint Retrospective

### Monthly
- Roadmap review
- Strategic alignment

## Success Metrics

- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Delivery predictability
- Alignment efficiency
- Reduced communication gaps
