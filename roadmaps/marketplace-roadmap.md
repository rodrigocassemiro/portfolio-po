# Marketplace Product Roadmap

## Vision

Build a seamless AI-powered real estate marketplace that simplifies the rental journey from discovery to contract signature.

## Phase 1 – Discovery & Validation

* Discovery & Research
* User Journey Mapping
* Stakeholder Alignment
* Business Requirements

### Goal

Validate business opportunities and customer needs before development.

---

## Phase 2 – Marketplace Foundation

* Marketplace MVP
* WhatsApp Authentication
* Property Search & Listings
* Property Details Experience

### Goal

Deliver the first version of the marketplace and validate customer engagement.

---

## Phase 3 – Customer Journey

* Visit Scheduling
* Post-Visit Feedback
* Credit Analysis Integration
* Digital Contract Signature

### Goal

Enable a complete digital rental experience.

---

## Phase 4 – Intelligence & Automation

* AI Automation Layer

### Goal

Reduce manual effort and improve scalability through intelligent automation.

---

## Phase 5 – Launch & Growth

* Go Live
* Optimization & Scaling

### Goal

Launch the platform, measure results and continuously improve performance.
